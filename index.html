Frequently Asked Questions

HOW MANY MEETINGS WILL I GET?

It varies. We focus on qualified leads, not just quantity. Current clients range from 4-20 meetings per month. We guarantee consistent prospecting efforts and continuous improvement.

DO I NEED A CONTRACT?

No formal contract, but a 6-month commitment is recommended to see results. This allows us to learn your business and optimize campaigns.

IS IT BETTER TO HIRE A SALESPERSON?

Consider the cost of recruitment, training, and ongoing management compared to our service, which offers predictability and expertise.

CAN WE WORK ON COMMISSION?

Commissions are not available initially due to upfront costs. We may revisit this after establishing a successful prospecting partnership and strategy with you.

Can you handle our niche industry?

Absolutely! We specialize in niche markets.

How do you find ideal clients?

We leverage a combination of tools, often starting with LinkedIn. We tailor the approach to your specific needs.

What makes you different from bad experiences with other appointment setters?

This industry can be unreliable and unpredictable. Were experienced B2B sales specialists with a transparent approach. We focus on quality, not just quantity, and continuously learn to improve.
